What's Beer Me all About?

Do you enjoy delicious craft beer? Are you in search of a local brewery, or maybe just a new spot to pass time with like-minded beer lovers? We have just the thing for you then...

WELCOME to BEER ME! From a love of beer and tech, we have collaborated to create something that any beer enthusiasts will love. Our goal is to enhance the community of craft beer enthusiasts so that more people get to experience great beer and the people who make it. Let us help you discover your new favorite local brewery!
